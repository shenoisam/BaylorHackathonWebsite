IEEE

IEEE is the professional association for Electrical Engineers

IEEE is a student-run organization promoting electrical engineering, with learning opportunities to see what engineers do in the workforce. We are vocationally minded with an emphasis on job-related tasks, but also involved in the collegiate atmosphere, hosting fun events at Baylor University. We strive to help members make the necessary connections with other students and professionals in order to succeed.
